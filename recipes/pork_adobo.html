Pork Belly Adobo

I am a proud Filipino who was raised on adobo. Pork adobo has to be my definitive favorite - especially if it is with pork belly. One of the best things about Filipino adobo is that everyone has their own recipe! Whether it's different ratios of ingredients or different ingredients used altogether, your experience of cooking adobo will be a delicious one. My take on adobo embraces the traditional recipe - building upon its essential flavors to create an elevated dish.

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Yellow Onion
  • Bay Leaves
  • Sugar
  • Chicken Bouillon
  • Dried Black Peppercorns
  • Star Anise
  • Cinammon Stick
